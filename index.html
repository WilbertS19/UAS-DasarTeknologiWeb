News

Ko Pin Yi

Ko has left an indelible mark on the world of pool after becoming the first Chinese Taipei player to clinch the World Pool Masters title in 2023. However, his most significant feat came in 2015 when he became a multiple world champion, winning the World Pool Championship, the World Cup of Pool, and the World Ten-Ball Championship..

Efren Reyes

Billiards icon Efren “Bata” Reyes was given rockstar treatment recently when he attended the 2024 World Pool Championship in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia. Widely regarded as the greatest pool player of all time, the 69-year-old cue artist nicknamed “The Magician” was seen being escorted inside the Prince Abdullah Al-Faisal City Sports Hall during the said nine-ball tournament..

Shane Van Boening

SVB has forged an illustrious career in professional pool, winning the 2022 World Pool Championship and five US Open titles. His recent victory in Premier League Pool 2024 further solidifies his status as one of the greats, alongside gaining recognition from Billiards Digest being named "Player of the Decade" for the 2010s..
